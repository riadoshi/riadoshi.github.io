Ria Doshi

Hi! I’m a fourth-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying computer science. My research focuses on building generalist robot policies, through cross-embodied data, vision-language pre-training, and autonomous policy learning. I’ve had the pleasure of working with Professor Sergey Levine and Professor Jeannette Bohg on the above. Aside from robot learning, I’ve done research in emergent phenomena of LLMs with Professor Anant Sahai. I’m also the author of The Code Detectives, a book series about young girls using AI to solve mysteries.
